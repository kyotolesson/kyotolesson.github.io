Make-up Class Arrangements

Students may join another class of their same level if the class is not full (of 8 students) and if the two classes have the same curriculum progression. Alternatively, students can explore Co-learning Hours.


Co-learning Hours

Co-learning Hours are only for term classes, meaning Kyoto Lesson’s weekly program. In the event of dispute, Kyoto Lesson holds the rights to the final decision. All Co-learning Hours quota needs to be taken during the same term the class is missed and cannot be carried forward to another term. Outstanding quota is forfeited after the last day of the term. It is the parent's responsibility to inform Kyoto Lesson (by registering on kyotolesson.com) before the start of the class in the event of absence. Walk-in to Co-learning Hours is not accepted. Kyoto Lesson does not accommodate rescheduling or Co-learning Hours request for notifications given less than 24 hours prior to the lesson or after lesson. Co-learning Hours is a self-guided session where students are given a lesson handout. Experienced instructors are on site to facilitate if students have any question. The whole period last for 60 minutes. A maximum of 2 Co-learning Hours quota is provided per 9-week to 10-week term, or 3 quota is provided per 14-week term. Co-learning Hours are subject to limited seats on a first-come first-serve basis. If the session is full, students can join next week’s session.
